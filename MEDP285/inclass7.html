HOW TO COOK RICE

TOOLS & INGREDIENTS


  • Water
  • 1 Cup of White Rice
  • Bowl
  • Finger
  • Stirring Tool

    • STEPS

    1. Pour however amount of rice into a bowl
    2. Rinse thoroughly.
      3. DONT over rinse.
    3. Pour cleaned rice into your rice cooker.
    4. Pour water into your rice cooker.
    5. Use your finger as an indicator to show you if there's too little or too much water.
    6. Close your rice cooker lid and then press your default cooking option on machine...easy!
