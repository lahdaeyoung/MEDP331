About


Austin Richard Post is an American Singer & Rapper. He was born in Syracuse, New York on July 4, 1995. He first became big and caught the attention of the world in 2015 with his song "White Iverson"

Net Worth


$30 Million

Achievements


  • Sold more than 60 million records in the US
  • Achieved Diamond certification from RIAA for "Congratulations"
  • 3-time AMA Winner, BMA Winner, and a MTV VMA Winner
  • Received 6 Grammy Nominations
